# AgentOps Cloud — 90-Second Voiceover Script

Team: **Brotherhood**
Target runtime: **1:30** at a natural, confident pace (~150–160 wpm). ~225 words.

---

**[0:00–0:08] — Hook**
> Every company building with AI now has an invisible problem: dozens — sometimes hundreds — of AI agents running across LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, custom code. And nobody in the building can tell you what they all are.

**[0:08–0:20] — Problem**
> Who owns this agent? What does it cost? What can it access? Which one is a security risk waiting to happen? Today, the honest answer is: nobody knows. There's no inventory, no control plane — just chaos, scaled across every team.

**[0:20–0:35] — Solution intro**
> This is AgentOps Cloud, built by team Brotherhood. Think Datadog, Okta, and Cloudflare — but for AI agents. One control plane that sits above every framework, instead of replacing any of them.

**[0:35–0:55] — Product / how it works**
> Connect a GitHub repo, or upload a manifest — our Enterprise Health Scan discovers your entire agent fleet in real time: framework, owner, cost, risk, health. It turns that into a nine-section executive report — security risks, cost drivers, optimization opportunities, a prioritized action plan — generated by transparent, explainable rules. Never a black box.

**[0:55–1:10] — Trust / differentiator**
> Every report is cryptographically sealed — hashed and anchored on-chain, on Base Sepolia — so any stakeholder can independently verify it was never tampered with. Login is wallet-native through OKX Wallet. No passwords, no third-party identity provider.

**[1:10–1:25] — Traction / close**
> We're live today. Real app, real API, real contracts on Base. Six pages, one job: give every enterprise a clear picture of its AI agent fleet — and a ranked list of what to fix first.

**[1:25–1:30] — CTA**
> AgentOps Cloud. Know your fleet. Trust your agents. Built by Brotherhood.

---

### Delivery notes
- Keep energy up through the problem section (0:00–0:20) — this is the tension the rest resolves.
- Slow down slightly on "hashed and anchored on-chain" and "wallet-native" — these are the credibility beats, don't rush them.
- Land the CTA with a beat of silence before "Built by Brotherhood."
